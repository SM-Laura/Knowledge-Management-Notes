Become a **Knowledge Architect**, Not Just a Writer 🧠

AI can generate text, but it struggles with **structuring complex knowledge ecosystems**.

Focus on:

- Designing **information architecture**
    
- Structuring documentation for **retrieval and reuse**
    
- Creating **taxonomy, metadata, and knowledge models**
    

Key skills:

- Knowledge graphs
    
- Content modeling
    
- Ontologies
    
- Structured documentation (DITA, Markdown systems, docs-as-code)
    

Why this matters:  
AI systems work best when **knowledge is well-structured**, and humans are still needed to design those systems.
