Specialize in **Complex or Regulated Domains** 📚

AI performs best with generic content. It struggles with **high-stakes, specialized knowledge**.

Consider deep expertise in areas like:

- Developer documentation
    
- APIs and SDKs
    
- Healthcare or medical documentation
    
- Cybersecurity
    
- Financial compliance
    
- Enterprise software
    

The more **domain expertise** you have, the harder you are to replace.
